Eduford

The first public institution of higher education in the US.
In its beginnings, our institution offered study programs in the field of Art and Mass Communication, constantly expanding with study programs in other fields, thus becoming a relevant academic center in the country and region and serving as a model institution of higher education. not only in the U.S. but also in the region. As a public institution, Eduford provided a genuine example of promoting institutional autonomy and academic freedom in teaching and scientific research within the institution.

Our history

Eduford is the largest investment in the country in the field of education. In the University Campus in New York there are 6 objects dedicated to the exercise of educational activities equipped with modern facilities for creative learning and contemporary technology. Within this campus, in addition to the sports recreation center that has a swimming pool, football, volleyball, tennis fields, etc., there is also a sports gym which enables the development of the social and sports life of college students.

Study Programs

Our institution offers study programs in various fields starting from Art and Mass Communication and expanding the offer to other fields. Computer Science, Law, Journalism, Political Science, Public Administration, Dentistry, Real Estate, and many other programs.

Institutional Autonomy

Eduford serves as an example of promoting institutional autonomy and academic freedom in teaching and research. Achieving the high level of universities also requires expansion in infrastructure, increasing funds and further increasing innovative partnerships with other European and world institutions.
